Why OptiPol

Restricted Resources

Police have had to deal with increasingly limited resources. Policing solutions that not only shows where crimes will happen, but also how to more effectively deploy units are necessary to make the unit deployment planning process faster and more efficient.

Public Perception of Discrimination

There's been high criticism of police work on the media and by the public, especially regarding police misconduct and ethnical discrimination. This is reflected in historical crime data, which in turns infects any intelligent predictive model with the same bias. Police departments need to control this bias and avoid perpetuating discriminatory behavior in future engagements.

Meet OptiPol

The solution to help police departments prevent crime more effectively and plan faster and fairer.

Reduce Discrimination

The fairness of your crime predictions and deployment plans are always there for you to check.

Plan Faster

Optipol leverages mathematical optimization techniques to prescribe you the more efficient deployment plans in seconds.

Ensure less discriminatory policing

Fairness Indicators

Fairness indicators in the crime prediction screen and patrol deployment screen help you ensure that the your predictive model's predictions and your unit deployment plan are not discriminatory.

Have a collection of different machine learning models to choose from, and switch between models on-the-fly. Train new models through an easy-to-use interface using a collection of additional features to remove bias and create fairer, less discriminatory models.
