Why OptiPol

Restricted Resources

Police have had to deal with increasingly limited resources. Policing solutions that not only shows where crimes will happen, but also how to more effectively deploy units are necessary to make the unit deployment planning process faster and more efficient.

Public Perception of Discrimination

There's been high criticism of police work on the media and by the public, especially regarding police misconduct and ethnical discrimination. This is reflected in historical crime data, which in turns infects any intelligent predictive model with the same bias. Police departments need to control this bias and avoid perpetuating discriminatory behavior in future engagements.
